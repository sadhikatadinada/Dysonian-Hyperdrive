ABOUT US

The Dysonsian Hyperdrive is the collaboration of Three services(the Dyson Sphere, Hoop-a-planet, flare) which aim to aid mankind with its future endeavors in space.


Flare is a service that aims to mine elements(metals and non metals) from asteroids which pass by the Earth closer than the moon.


Hoop-a-Planet (H-a-P) is a revolutionary Transportation System which will change the way people commute including moving from one planet to another


It is an immense artificial construct orbiting a star with the capability to capture its light and convert it into useful forms of energy.


All the Services when combined can yield even better results for humanity, i.e. Interstellar Travel may finally be possible.
